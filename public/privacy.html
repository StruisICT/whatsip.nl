Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-06-22

What we do

whatsip.nl shows you your own public IP address and related network metadata (such as your ISP/ASN and approximate country), read at Cloudflare's edge for the sole purpose of displaying it back to you. We do not store your IP address or any of this information.

Cookies

We do not set any cookies ourselves. A colour-theme preference is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and never leaves your device.

Advertising

This site shows ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve and measure ads. You can manage your ad personalisation in your Google Ad Settings, and learn more at Google's advertising policies.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on the project repository.
